Maps & Geography Index

Textual guides to biblical geography. You can add SVG/PNG maps later; these overviews give coordinates in Scripture.

Patriarchal Journeys

Ur → Haran → Canaan; Egypt and return (Gen 11–13; 46–50). Key sites: Shechem, Bethel, Hebron, Beersheba.

Exodus & Wilderness

From Goshen to Sinai to the plains of Moab (Exod 1–19; Num 10–36). Sinai theophany; tabernacle.

United & Divided Kingdoms

Jerusalem as capital; division into Israel (Samaria) and Judah (Jerusalem). Exile routes to Assyria/Babylon.

Jesus’ Ministry Regions

Galilee (Capernaum), Samaria, Judea (Jerusalem). Key sites: Nazareth, Bethany, Bethsaida, Caesarea Philippi.

Pauline Missions

Acts 13–21 itineraries: Cyprus, Pisidia, Macedonia, Achaia, Asia Minor; Rome (Acts 28).
