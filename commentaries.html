Commentary Notes (Concise, Scholarly)

Non‑denominational, exegetical summaries to aid study. Each note highlights structure, setting, and key theology.

Genesis

Setting: Primeval and patriarchal history. Themes: Creation, fall, covenant, promise. Structure: Toledoth sections. Key: Gen 12:1–3; 15; 22.

Isaiah

Setting: 8th–7th c. BC Judah. Themes: Holiness, judgment, hope, Servant. Key: Isa 6; 9; 11; 40–55; 53.

Matthew

Setting: First‑century Jewish context. Themes: Kingdom, fulfillment, discipleship. Key: Sermon on the Mount; parables; 28:18–20.

Romans

Setting: Corinth, AD 57. Themes: Sin, justification, sanctification, Israel, life in the Spirit. Key: 1:16–17; 3:21–26; 8; 12.

Hebrews

Setting: Persecuted believers; anonymous author. Themes: Christ’s supremacy, new covenant, perseverance. Key: 1:1–4; 4:14–16; 8–10; 12.
