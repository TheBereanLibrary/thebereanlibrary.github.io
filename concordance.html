Concordance (Key Theological Terms)

Curated English terms with Greek/Hebrew anchors and Scripture lists to support doctrine. Non‑denominational, Scripture‑centered.

Grace — χάρις (charis)

Unmerited favor of God effectual in salvation and sanctification.

  • Eph 2:8–9; Rom 3:24; Titus 2:11–14; 2 Cor 12:9

Justification — δικαίωσις / δικαιόω (dikaiōsis / dikaioō)

Judicial declaration of righteousness by faith in Christ apart from works.

  • Rom 3:21–26; 4:5; 5:1; Gal 2:16; Phil 3:9

Sanctification — ἁγιασμός (hagiasmos)

Set-apart status in Christ and progressive growth in holiness.

  • 1 Thess 4:3–8; Rom 6; John 17:17; Heb 12:14

Redemption — ἀπολύτρωσις (apolytrōsis)

Deliverance by a price; accomplished through Christ’s atoning death.

  • Mark 10:45; Eph 1:7; Col 1:13–14; Heb 9:12

Kingdom of God — βασιλεία τοῦ θεοῦ

God’s reign inaugurated by Christ and consummated at His return.

  • Matt 4:17; 6:33; Luke 17:20–21; Rev 11:15

Faith — πίστις (pistis)

Trusting reliance on Christ that receives God’s promises.

  • Rom 3:28; Gal 2:20; Heb 11; Eph 2:8

Repentance — μετάνοια (metanoia)

Spirit‑wrought change of mind/heart that turns from sin to God.

  • Mark 1:15; Acts 2:38; 20:21; 2 Cor 7:9–10

Adoption — υἱοθεσία (huiothesia)

Believers made sons and daughters in Christ.

  • Rom 8:14–17; Gal 4:4–7; Eph 1:5

Atonement

Christ’s substitutionary sacrifice satisfying divine justice and reconciling sinners.

  • Isa 53; Rom 3:25–26; 2 Cor 5:21; 1 Pet 2:24; Heb 9–10
